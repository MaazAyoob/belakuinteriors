How It Works

Your Journey to a
Dream Home

A seamless, transparent five-step process — designed so you're always informed, always in control, and never surprised.

01
Meet Our Designer

A complimentary, no-obligation consultation at your home or our studio. We listen to your vision, take measurements, and understand your lifestyle — no templates, no shortcuts.

02
Sign with Belaku

Receive a detailed scope of work with itemised pricing. Our agreements are transparent — fixed price, fixed timeline. No hidden charges, ever. Sign digitally in minutes.

03
Design Phase

Our designers create photorealistic 3D renders of every room. You select materials, finishes, and hardware from our premium catalogue. Revisions are included — we don't move forward until you love it.

04
Execute & Install

Our trained craftsmen take over. Civil work, joinery, electrical, painting, and installation — all coordinated by a dedicated Site Manager. Weekly photo updates sent to you directly.

05
Settle In

Detailed handover walkthrough, snag list completion, and a comprehensive 10-year warranty document. Our after-service team remains available long after you move in.

Belaku (ಬೆಳಕು) means "light" in Kannada. Since 2023, we have brought that spirit — warmth, clarity, and radiance — into over 300 homes and offices across Bangalore.
